2.1 Basic Sense of Risks and Management

Created2023-11-19|Updated2024-04-28|FRMPart1Foundations
|Word Count:1.2k|Reading Time:5mins

What Is Risk?

Possibility of the bad things that might happen

It exists everywhere and anytime, we will focus on the financial world.

But it is not always a bad thing

  • Good risk: Risks that have a positive expected payoff on a stand alone basis.

  • Bad risk: Risks that can be expected to destroy value on a stand alone basis【基于单独、独立的基础或条件】.

bad risk:operational risk

  • Banks and other financial institutions cannot succeed without taking risks.

What Is Risk Management?

Risk management

how firms actively select the types and levels of risks that are appropriate for them to take.

一个公司可以决定自己承担哪些类型的风险和承担多大程度的风险。

Risk management and risk taking aren’t opposites, but two sides of the same coin.

  • Together they drive all our modern economies.

  • It’s all about making forward-looking choices about risk in relation to reward.【关键是在风险与回报之间做出具有前瞻性的选择。】

“It’s all about” 是一个常用的语法结构，指的是某事的核心或关键所在。它说明后面提到的内容是最重要的或最关键的方面。在给定的句子中，“It’s all about making forward-looking choices about risk in relation to reward” 意味着在与回报相关的风险方面做出前瞻性的选择是最重要的或关键的事情。换句话说，这是解决问题或取得成功的关键所在。

Is Risk Management Useful?

Challenges for risk management

 Fail to prevent market disruptions or accounting scandals.

A market disruption is a situation wherein markets cease to function in a regular manner, typically characterized by rapid and large market declines.

market disruptions：大盘下跌

accounting scandals:财务造假

 Derivative markets make it easier to take on large amount of risk.

衍生品具有更大的杠杆。

 Sophisticated financial engineering leads to the violent implosion of firms.

Sophisticated【工程上地高深地、复杂的】

 Only transfer risks to other firms.

  • Risk management is a zero-sum game

风险只能转移，不能消灭。

 Work to the short-term benefit【为了短期利益】

Risk Management Process

  1. Identify the risk

  2. Analyze and measure risk

  3. Assess the effects of all risk, and balance between risk and reward

  4. Manage the risk using different kind of tools

risk exposures:风险敞口

shift：转移

第一象限：高频高损类事件——风险规避——不做这类事件

第二象限：低频高损类事件——风险转移——购买保险

第三象限：低频低损类事件——保留——不做操作

第四象限：高频低损类事件——风险缓释——风险管理

Identify the risk

There are things we know that we know.

There are known unknowns… But there are also unknown unknowns.

已知风险：expected loss,unexpected loss

konwn unknowns:已经知道风险，但不了解风险机理，进而无法管理。

unknown unknowns:既不知道是什么，也不知道会产生的影响。

FRM只研究EL和UL

Analyze and measure risk

Quantitative Risk Measures

VaR(Value at risk)

 Developed by Dennis Weatherstone, CEO of J.P. Morgan

 VaR uses the loss distribution associated with a position or portfolio to estimate losses at a given level of likelihood (or confidence).

E.g. a one day 99% confidence level VaR of USD 1 million【一天内，有99%的概率至多产生1m的损失；一天内，有1%的概率至少损失1m】

Qualitative Risk Measures

 Scenario analysis【情景分析】

 Stress testing【压力测试】

银行一般使用99%或99.9%,保险公司使用99.5%

tail loss:就是 extreme loss

Expected Loss(EL)

EL is the average loss expected to incur from a position or a portfolio. It can be estimated by historical data of a period of time.

 It can be treated as a predictable expense rather than a risk or uncertainty

In the case of the credit risk of a loan:

 EL=EAD×LGD×PD

 PD: Probability of default【损失的概率】

 EAD: Exposure amount at default【贷款金额】

 LGD: Loss given default【在损失的条件下损失的比率】

EL is often priced into the product directly.

Unexpected Loss(UL)

On a bad day, losses can range above the expected level. Unexpected loss is the surprising loss that above the EL in bad days.

  • Value at risk (VaR) uses the loss distribution to estimate losses at a given level of likelihood(confidence).

  • UL= VaR - EL

  • Economic capital(usually called risk capital) is the amount of liquid capital used to cover unexpected loss.

Basel协议规定经济资本>8%

Correlation risk【祸不单行的风险】, where unfavorable events happen together, will drive potential losses to unexpected levels.

In practice, concentration【集中】 makes the probability of multiple losses high for a portfolio. As a result, we need more capital to cover the UL.

Tail Risk

Some credit portfolios, however, exhibit a much more extreme variance in their losses over longer intervals of time (e.g., a decade).

From the crisis lesson, we focus on the tail risk beyond confidence level【高于置信水平】 which cannot be explained by VaR.

Var值不能解释tail risk

  • Extreme value theory(EVT) focuses on tail distribution to understand the black swans

  • Expected shortfall is the EL of tail distribution【尾部损失的平均数ES】

Balance Between Risk and Reward

There is a natural trade-off【平衡】 between risk and reward: the more risk takes, the more potential reward is earned.

However, there are challenges.

  • Conflicts between EL and UL, can be solved by RAROC

    • Risk adjusted return on capital (RAROC)=after-tax risk-adjusted expected return(EL is subtracted)/economic capital

  • Correlation between risk factors is changing

风险之间的相关系数会变化。

  • Conflicts of interests among different units【不同部门】, can be solved by three lines of defense【三道防线】

    • business line that generates, owns, and manages risk;

    业务部门自己管理风险。

    • risk managers implement day-to-day oversight;

    风险管理部门监督。

    • periodic independent oversight【监督】 and assurance【保证】, such as internal audit

    阶段性独立的内部审计。
