Measurement Bases of Assets and Liabilities

Intangible Assets

确认无形资产的的条件

It can be separated or divided from the enterprise and can be used for sale, transfer, licensing, lease or exchange alone or together with relevant contracts, assets or liabilities.

它可以与企业分开或划分，并可以单独或与相关合同、资产或负债一起用于销售、转让、许可、租赁或交换。

Derived from contractual rights or other legal rights.

它源自合同权利或其他法律权利。

无形资产的种类

licenses, patents, copyrights, trademarks, franchises, future advertising benefits, etc.

各种类型的无形资产如许可证、专利、版权、商标、特许经营权、未来广告收益等。

Goodwill, brand value, customer relationships are assets but not identifiable. To be identifiable, the asset should be separable from the enterprise and derived from legal rights.

商誉，品牌价值和客户关系是资产，但不具备可识别性。要具备可识别性，资产应该能够与企业分开，并源自法律权利。

研发费用

Research phase expenses are not capitalized while development phase expenses can be capitalized if they meet certain criteria like feasibility.

研究阶段的费用不计入资本支出，而开发阶段的费用则可以根据可行性等特定标准进行资本化。

Research phase: seek to find new knowledge. IAS No. 38 prohibits the capitalization of costs as intangible assets during the research phase. Instead, these costs must be expensed on the income statement.

Development phase : design or testing of prototypes and models . IAS Costs incurred in the development stage can be capitalized as intangible assets if certain criteria are met, including technological feasibility, the ability to use or sell the resulting asset, and the ability to complete the project.

U.S. GAAP prohibits the capitalization as an asset of almost all research and development costs.

必须费用化的支出

Internally generated brands【内部形成的品牌】, mastheads【头部标题】, publishing titles【出版物名称】, customer lists【客户名单】, etc.

Start - up costs【创业成本】.

Training costs【培训费用】.

Administrative and other general overhead costs【管理和其他一般经营支出】.

Advertising and promotion【营销费用】.

Relocation and reorganization expenses【迁址和重组费用】.

Redundancy and other termination costs【解雇和其他终止费用】.

Long Term Liabilities

(1) Analyst need to analyze the interest rate

Reason that affect the interest rate:

  1. Credit rating: better the rating, lower the interest rate

  2. Whether use collateral

  3. Reasonable intention of borrowing

  4. Operating capacity of the management team.

  5. Risk and return of the project.

To control for the financial risk, institutions declined to provide long-term debt in recent years.

(2) Kinds of collateral

  1. Guarantee【担保】: Involvement of a third party

  2. Mortgage【抵押】: use non-current asset like PPE

  3. Pledge【质押】: typical use current asset like stocks

From liquidity aspect, the guarantee can be better than others. Then Pledge and mortgage.

Payback capacity

Current ratio=current asset/current liability

If the ratio<1, short term payback capacity has problem.

On average, 1.22 (exclude financial firms)

Quick ratio=current asset-inventory/current liability

Quick is faster than current, because of inventory, on average 0.5

Cash ratio=monetary capital/current liability

interest coverage ratio=EBT/interest payment

Leverage ratio=total leverage/total asset

Case:

In order to cooperate with financing (IPO), enterprises may have the impulse to increase profits by raising the price.

Similarly, before bond financing, companies may also use the same motivation to whitewash 【粉饰】their financial statements.

Case from Chaori bond default, the first public bond default on Chinese financial market. On March 7, 2012, Chaori Sun publicly issued 1 billion yuan corporate bonds on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, with a coupon rate of 8.98%, a term of 5 years, and a bond rating of AA.

Listed in 2010.

Decreasing Forecasted total Net income is due to cold winter period of photovoltaic industry, the bond’s rating is AA.

After bond issuing

A month has passed. During this month, according to the company announcement from Super Sun, the annual report originally scheduled to be disclosed on March 28th has been delayed. According to the company’s announcement on March 26th, the disclosure of the 2011 annual report has been postponed to April 26th.

Delaying the disclosure date: often means that auditors feel that the audit risk is high and do not agree with the company’s current financial statements.

During this period, two independent directors of the company also submitted resignations, and the company announcement stated that it was due to personal reasons.

One possibility is that the two independent directors feel the risk is too high and stop working.

The main reason for the sudden change in performance is that Chaori Sun did not include the several overseas subsidiaries it invested in in the consolidation report.

Before consolidation, all sales of the parent company to the subsidiaries were counted as income. When these subsidiaries were included in the consolidation report, internal transactions within the group were offset, resulting in a decrease in revenue scale and profits.

Chaori Sun has committed significant omissions, false statements, and exaggerated profit forecasts in its Corporate Bond Prospectus.

AA at the end of 2012, BBB in April 2013, and CCC in May 2013, *ST after 2013.

Components of Equity

Capital contributed by owners

Capital Preferred shares【优先股】 have rights that take precedence over the rights of common shareholders — rights that generally pertain to 【关于】receipt of dividends (not always cumulative if omitted by the board of directors) and receipt of assets if the company is liquidated.

Different compared with those shares repurchased (treasury stock) by the company (IPO, SEO increase, Repurchase decrease).

The profit realized by the enterprise does not belong to the employees of the enterprise or the creditor. It belongs to shareholders. Therefore, the profit realized by the enterprise belongs to the owner’s equity.

A certain percentage of the profits for long-term development of an enterprise is surplus reserves.

Therefore, the surplus reserve in the owner’s equity of the enterprise is 50000 yuan (盈余公积); The remaining profit is also given a share in shareholders’ equity, which is called undistributed profit (未分配利润 ). Therefore, the enterprise’s undistributed profit in owner’s equity is 450000 yuan

Minority interest

Minority interest (or noncontrolling interest) . The equity interests of minority shareholders in the subsidiary companies that have been consolidated by the parent (controlling) company but that are not wholly owned by the parent company.

Treasury stock

Treasury stock (or own shares repurchased) . The repurchase of company shares may occur when management considers the shares undervalued or when it wants to limit the effects of dilution from various employee stock compensation plans. Treasury stock is a reduction of shareholders ’ equity and a reduction of total shares outstanding. Treasury shares are nonvoting and do not receive dividends if declared by the company.

Comprehensive income

Comprehensive income =Net income + Other comprehensive income

Unrealized changes in the market value of marketable securities classified as ‘‘available for sale,’’ unrealized changes in the market value of hedged financial instruments and derivatives classified as cash flow hedges, unrealized foreign currency translation adjustments, and certain changes in pension and other post-employment benefit assets and liabilities appear in other comprehensive income, net of their tax effects. These items usually give rise to deferred tax assets or deferred tax liabilities because the income tax law includes such gains and losses in taxable income when realized.

Thus, a portion of the change in deferred tax assets and liabilities on the balance sheet does not flow through income tax expense on the income statement

Capital Structure Analysis

Whether the debt’s term structure is consistent with the asset term structure

Use long-term debt (use shareholder’s equity and non-current debt) to increase long-term asset.

Use short-term debt to support seasonable volatility in current asset.

Analyst need to check the relative increase of short term asset, long-term asset and relative increase in short-term debt and long-term debt.

Avoid use short-term debt to support increase of long-term asset.

Leverage ratio

Analyst need to pay attention to the leverage ratio: Too large leverage ratio decrease capacity to pay principal and interest rate. Loss to the debtholders. Lower the flexibility of financing

【有时对于集团公司来说，母公司可能会建立一个资金池，以共同为子公司筹集资金，以提高效率。因此，母公司的利息支付可能较高。】
