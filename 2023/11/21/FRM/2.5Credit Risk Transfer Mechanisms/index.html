2.5CreditRiskTransferMechanisms

发表于2023-11-21|更新于2023-11-21|FRM
|字数总计:548|阅读时长:2分钟

Traditional Credit Risk Mitigation Approaches

Traditional transfer of credit risk:

  • Requiring collaterals

  • Purchasing insurance from third-party counterparties

  • Netting【净额结算】 of exposures 【风险敞口净额结算】to counterparties

净额结算可以防止A把所有的欠款给B后，B拒不归还任何对A的欠款，从而给A造成较大损失。

  • Marking to market 【盯市】/ Margining【保证金】

marking to market:盯市，分为逐月、逐日、逐周盯市。

  • Termination by a set of trigger events

一系列事件的触发将终止合约。

  • Reassignment of a credit exposure【信贷风险】 to another party

把A欠自己的钱卖给B。

  • Syndication

音译：辛迪加，国内称为“银团贷款”。

Credit Derivatives

Credit derivatives enables institutions to effectively tailor pools of credit-risk exposures by repackaging of risk.

Risk transfer

  • credit default swaps(CDS)

Securitization

  • collateralized debt obligations (CDOs)

  • collateralized loan obligations (CLOs).

Credit Default Swaps

CDS:In a Credit Default Swaps contract, a protection buyer (say A) pays a

premium to the protection seller (say B), in exchange for payment if a

credit event occurs.

就是一般意义上的保险。

Securitization

Securitization: Securitization involves the repackaging of loans and other assets into new securities that can then be sold in the securities markets.

mortgage:按揭

proceeds:收款，款项；

originator：发起人——银行。

SPV：特殊目的载体；

credit tranching:信用分级

From Buy-and-hold to Originate-to-distribute

The shift toward the OTO business model seemed to offer the financial services industry many benefits

  • Originators benefited from greater capital efficiency【资本使用效益】 (by Basel capital adequacy requirements【资本充足要求】) and enhanced funding opportunities【融资机会】, as well as lower earnings volatility

  • Investors benefited from a wider array of investments, allowing them

to diversify their portfolios and better sync their risk/return profiles

  • Borrowers benefited from the expansion of available credit and product options, as well as from the lower borrowing costs.

银行拥有更多的资金，愿意以更低的利息借款，会引起信贷扩张。

The Securitization Products

abcp:资产支持商业票据

CDO：二次资产证券化的ABS产品

CLO：二次资产证券化的LBS

CMO：二次资产证券化的：MBS

Issues Addressed in Securitization

Securitization amplified systemic risk during the crisis by allowing massive leverage and risk concentration in the financial sector

  • Loan origination: compensation was tied to high loan volumes

  • Securitization: the risk embedded was not transparent for investors

  • Credit rating【信用评级】: overreliance【过度信赖】 on the accuracy and transparency of credit ratings

  • Risk management: poor risk management in many segment (e.g.,assessment, stress test)

  • Risk concentration potential

  • High complexity

MBS具有较高的资产集中性。

buy and hold:买入并持有。对立面就是securitization。
